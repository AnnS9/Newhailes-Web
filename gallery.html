The Owners

The Newhailes estate was inhabited by many lords and barons, military men and artists. Each of them left a trace in the form of modernization and renovation. The first owner and architect of this place was 2nd Lord Bellenden who was Scottish nobleman, architect. Due to financial difficulties, an unsuccessful coal-mining venture forced Smith to sell the house just a decade or so later. The last occupants of the house were Sir Mark Dalrymple, 3rd Baronet and Antonia Marian Amy Isabel Stewart He served with Royal Scots Territorials, during the Second World War, joined the Royal Air Force. Mark married Antonia Marian Amy Isabel Stewart, in 1946. They had no children and when Mark died in June 1971 the Barony became extinct. The house became vacant around 1980.
In 2011 at the age of 71 the widowed Lady Antonia left her apartment in the house and went to live in a cottage on the estate.
